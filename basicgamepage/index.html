Subnautica

Publishing Info
Published by: Unknow World Entertainment, Inc. and Perfect World Co.
Developed by: Unkown World Entertainment, Inc
Released by: December 16. 2014

Description

Subnautica is an open-world survival game in which a player takes on the role of a terraformer engineer,
having escaped the starship Aurora just before it mysteriously crashes onto an alien planet.
The planet is mostly covered by water and the player must adapt to this new alien environment,
gathering food, building a habitat and discovering the secrets to why the ship crashed.

Game Catergories

  • Genre: Action/Adventure
  • Perspective: 1st-Person
  • Visual: Dynamic
  • Gameplay: Survival
