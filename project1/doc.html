Goal of website

My goal for this website was to create a film review/ animation blog post.
I wanted to emulate a traditional review page from a website like Polygon or IGN, but covering an animated movie rather than a video game.
The users of this website would most likely be people are intrested in animation.
Similarly to how people who read IGN or Polygon are people who are in the sphere of video games.
This is targeted towards people who are either in the world of animtion or simply just enjoy the media.

Additional Notes

I am happy with how the website came out. There are a few things I would change, such as adding the ability to
close the menu on mobile rather than it just being open. I would also change how the navbar scrolls down. Adding a
top bar that moves with it and pushes the text along it so as to make it cleaner.
I would also reformat some of the text to be less big chunks
